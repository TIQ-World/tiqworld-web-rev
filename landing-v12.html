The Problem We're Solving

The NDT industry runs on paper, phone calls, and word-of-mouth. That's about to change.

Every year, thousands of NDT professionals struggle to prove their competency to employers. Their certifications are scattered across filing cabinets. Their skills are self-reported on generic job platforms that don't understand the difference between UT Level II and PAUT.

Meanwhile, manufacturers and EPC firms spend weeks vetting candidates manually — checking each certification, calling references, reviewing work history — only to find that half the applicants don't meet their specific code requirements.

Training providers are disconnected from the hiring pipeline. Students complete courses without visibility into which employers need their newly certified skills. And the entire ecosystem operates in silos.

Our Answer

One verified, intelligent platform that connects every stakeholder in quality.

TIQ World replaces fragmented workflows with a single digital layer — verified credentials, AI-powered matching, certified training, and compliance-ready documentation. All in one place.
