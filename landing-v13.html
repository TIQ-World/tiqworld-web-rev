FAQ

Common Questions

What is TIQ World?
TIQ World is a precision platform connecting NDT (Non-Destructive Testing) professionals, training providers, manufacturers, and recruiters. Incubated at IIT Madras and developed within the Centre for Non-Destructive Evaluation (CNDE), it's the digital foundation for manufacturing quality assurance.
Who is TIQ World for?
Four audiences: Professionals (NDT inspectors seeking jobs and certifications), Recruiters (HR leads hiring verified talent), Training Providers (institutes listing courses), and Manufacturers (OEMs/EPC firms needing on-demand inspection workforce).
What NDT methods are covered?
All 8 major NDT methods: Ultrasonic Testing (UT), Radiographic Testing (RT), Magnetic Particle Testing (MT), Liquid Penetrant Testing (PT), Eddy Current Testing (ET), Visual Inspection (VT), Phased Array UT (PAUT), and Time of Flight Diffraction (TOFD). Covering ASNT, PCN, and BINDT standards across Oil & Gas, Aerospace, Power, and Infrastructure sectors.
How does AI skill verification work?
Our adaptive assessment engine generates expert-reviewed questions for each NDT discipline and level. Professionals complete proctored online sessions, and results are mapped against live industry standards — providing verified competency records without human bias or paper checklists.
Is TIQ World free for professionals?
Creating a profile and browsing opportunities is free. Training enrolments have a course fee set by the training provider, plus a convenient fee and payment gateway charges (transparently shown before checkout). Enterprise features have separate pricing.
