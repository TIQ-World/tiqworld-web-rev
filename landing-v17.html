Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about TIQWorld — answered.

01 What is TIQWorld?
TIQWorld is a precision platform connecting NDT & QC professionals, training providers, manufacturers, and recruiters. Incubated at IIT Madras and developed within the Centre for Non-Destructive Evaluation (CNDE), TIQWorld is the digital foundation for manufacturing quality assurance.
02 Who is TIQWorld for?
Four audiences: NDT/QA/QC Professionals seeking jobs and certifications, HR & Talent Leads hiring verified talent, Training Institutes & Trainers listing courses, and QA/QC Heads of OEMs & EPC Firms needing inspection workforce and AI-powered reporting.
03 What are the types of reports that can be made using AI Report Studio?
Presently, inspection reports for NDT methods UT, RT, MT, PT, ET, VT, PAUT, TOFD — covering ASNT, PCN, and BINDT standards across Oil & Gas, Aerospace, Power, and Manufacturing sectors. In future, there will be many more report types.
04 How does AI job matching work?
TIQWorld's Smart CV engine parses your verified skills and certifications and matches you to active job postings. Job seekers receive tailored alerts — including training courses aligned to career growth. Recruiters receive a shortlisted set of relevant candidates for every posting in minutes.
05 Is TIQWorld free for professionals?
Creating a profile and browsing opportunities is free. Training enrolments have a course fee set by the training provider, plus a convenience fee and payment gateway charges — transparently shown before checkout. Enterprise features have separate pricing.
