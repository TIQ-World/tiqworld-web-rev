Common Questions

FAQ

What is TIQWorld?
TIQWorld is a precision platform connecting NDT (Non-Destructive Testing) professionals, training providers, manufacturers, and recruiters. Incubated at IIT Madras and developed within the Centre for Non-Destructive Evaluation (CNDE), TIQWorld is the digital foundation for manufacturing quality assurance.
Who is TIQWorld for?
Four audiences: Professionals (NDT inspectors seeking jobs and certifications), Recruiters (HR leads hiring verified talent), Training Providers (institutes listing courses), and Manufacturers (OEMs/EPC firms needing on-demand inspection workforce).
What NDT methods does TIQWorld cover?
All 8 major NDT methods: Ultrasonic Testing (UT), Radiographic Testing (RT), Magnetic Particle Testing (MT), Liquid Penetrant Testing (PT), Eddy Current Testing (ET), Visual Inspection (VT), Phased Array UT (PAUT), and Time of Flight Diffraction (TOFD). Covering ASNT, PCN, and BINDT standards across Oil & Gas, Aerospace, Power, and Infrastructure sectors.
How does AI skill verification work on TIQWorld?
TIQWorld's adaptive assessment engine generates expert-reviewed questions for each NDT discipline and level. Professionals complete proctored online sessions, and results are mapped against live industry standards — providing verified competency records without human bias or paper checklists.
Is TIQWorld free for professionals?
Creating a profile and browsing opportunities on TIQWorld is free. Training enrolments carry a course fee set by the training provider. Enterprise features for recruiters and manufacturers have separate pricing — contact us for details.
How is TIQWorld different from a general job board?
TIQWorld is purpose-built for NDT and manufacturing quality. Unlike general job boards, every profile on TIQWorld includes verified competency records, certification history, and AI-assessed skill scores — giving recruiters and manufacturers real signal, not just CVs.
